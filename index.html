Our Mission

Inspired by the Word of God, we proclaim the Gospel and demonstrate living faith through compassionate action—bridging the gap between churches and mission fields.

Proclaim the Gospel

We make disciples in obedience to the Great Commission with humility, love, and truth.

Serve the Vulnerable

We provide compassionate help—feeding, temporary basic needs, and practical care where it’s needed most.

Train, Send, Support

We train and support missionaries through logistical, financial, and strategic support—so the Gospel can go further.

Programs

We bridge the gap between churches and mission fields through training, sending, and support—logistical, financial, and strategic.

Homeless Outreach

Feeding, providing temporary basic needs, and offering compassionate support with dignity and prayer.

Missionary Training & Sending

Preparing and sending missionaries with practical training, spiritual formation, and ongoing care.

Missionary Support

Supporting missionaries through salaries, housing needs, travel, supplies, and field logistics.

Church Planting & Discipleship

Establishing churches, strengthening leaders, and making disciples in obedience to the Great Commission.

Education & Medical Care

Supporting education initiatives and basic medical care efforts in partnership with local communities.

Disaster Relief & Poverty Assistance

Responding to urgent needs with relief, resources, and long-term support for communities in crisis.
